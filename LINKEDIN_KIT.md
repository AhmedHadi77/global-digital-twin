## 5. Launch Post

I'm excited to share one of my recent full-stack projects: **Global Digital Twin**.

It's a real-time monitoring platform for simulated industrial devices, built with:

- Next.js
- Node.js and Express
- PostgreSQL
- Socket.IO
- Three.js

The platform includes:

- live telemetry for multiple devices
- anomaly detection and alert history
- KPI dashboard and device detail pages
- failure simulation controls
- interactive 3D device visualization

One of the most interesting parts was solving deployment constraints on free hosting. Since a separate background worker was not available, I redesigned the simulator to run inside the backend process and added HTTP polling fallback to keep the hosted demo reliable.
